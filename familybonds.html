FamilyBonds

About Us:

FamilyBonds is a group of blood related sisters and cousins focused on enriching our community by catering and decorating events & teaching children between the ages of 5 & up to cook exclusively in the microwave.

Why did we choose this approach?

Children within our communities are hungry and consuming unhealthy foods at an alarming rate, some of the time it is just a matter of parents being overworked and too exhausted to prepare the meals.

What we hope to give from our initiative?

We hope to teach young children to safely and self-sufficiently create individual and family meals with or "without" supervision, while expressing our creative culinary and crafting skills.

Who we are inspired by?

Our grandmother Juanita, who taught us from young ages to navagate the culinary world right in her kitchen.
